Pre-Construction Services

COMPLETE PRE-CONSTRUCTION SOLUTIONS

At The Leading Takeoff, we go beyond estimating. Our pre-construction services are designed to help contractors, developers, and architects prepare every project for success before breaking ground. From permit-ready drawings to full BIM models, we provide the documentation and planning your project needs.

Permit Drawings

We prepare complete permit-ready architectural and structural drawing sets that meet local building department requirements. Our drawings are designed for fast approval and smooth permit submission, helping you avoid costly delays.

3D Renderings

Our photorealistic 3D renderings bring your project to life before construction begins. Ideal for client presentations, marketing materials, and design approvals. We produce both exterior and interior renderings for residential and commercial projects.

BIM Modeling

We create detailed Building Information Models (BIM) using Revit for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. Our BIM models improve coordination, reduce errors, and provide a reliable foundation for all project stakeholders.

Shop Drawings

Our team produces accurate shop drawings for structural steel, millwork, precast concrete, MEP systems, and more. Shop drawings are reviewed for coordination and compliance before submission to the engineer of record.

As-Built Drawings

We convert field measurements and redlines into clean, accurate as-built drawings that document the final constructed condition of your project for future reference, permits, and renovations.

Project Scheduling

We develop detailed construction schedules using industry-standard tools including Primavera P6 and Microsoft Project. Our schedules cover phasing, milestones, critical path, and resource allocation to keep your project on track.
