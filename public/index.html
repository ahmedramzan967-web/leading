WHAT WE OFFER

Our Pre-Construction & Drafting Services

We offer contractors and developers an integrated suite of drafting and modeling services, customized to bring your designs from paper sketches to ready-to-build, approved plans.

Service 01

Permit Drawings

Millimeter-precise architectural and structural plans prepared for local municipal building department submittals, engineered to meet strict local building codes.

Service 02

Project Scheduling

Comprehensive Critical Path Method (CPM) baseline schedules developed in Primavera P6 and MS Project, optimizing timelines, tracking resource dependencies, and defending against delay claims.

Service 03

3D Renderings

High-fidelity, photo-realistic CGI interior and exterior architectural renderings that assist stakeholders in visualizing the final project and securing buy-in.

Service 04

BIM Modeling (Revit)

Clash-detection integrated multi-dimensional building modeling using Revit and Navisworks, preventing field interference and speeding up fabrication.

Service 05

Shop Drawings

Highly detailed manufacturing blueprints and assembly layout cards for structural steel fabrication, detailed millwork, and custom mechanical duct routings.

Service 06

As-Built Drawings

Meticulous post-construction documentation updating blueprints to mirror exactly what was built, crucial for future building renovations and maintenance.
